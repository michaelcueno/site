Initial Design

After our first meeting we have decided to use Matt's design as a starting point for our application. We all really liked the idea of the app 'being' the kitchen. The initial screen the user is faced with will be the blinds upon which each blind will represent one user in the hosehold. Upon tapping on a user, the blinds scroll up and give the user access to the app's user functions.
